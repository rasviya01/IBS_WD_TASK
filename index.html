About Us

Welcome to Maple Guest House, your cozy home away from home. Nestled among the beautiful maple trees, our guest house offers a peaceful and comfortable stay in a relaxed, friendly atmosphere. Whether you're here to unwind or explore, we provide a warm, welcoming space to help you feel right at home.

Our rooms are designed with your comfort in mind, offering a peaceful escape with all the modern amenities you need. From soft bedding to friendly service, we want every guest to feel relaxed and cared for during their stay. We’re here to make sure you enjoy a stress-free experience, whether you're relaxing by the window or taking in the natural beauty around you. Located in a quiet and scenic area, Maple Guest House is the perfect spot for those looking for a restful retreat.We can’t wait to welcome you and make your stay special!
